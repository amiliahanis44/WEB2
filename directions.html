Drive Directions from Petaling Jaya

Step Instruction
1 Start on Jalan Damansara: Head east on Jalan Damansara toward Jalan Semantan.
2 Merge onto Jalan Tun Razak: Continue straight to stay on Jalan Damansara, then take the ramp to Jalan Tun Razak.
3 Continue on Jalan Tun Razak: Drive along Jalan Tun Razak, passing through the city center.
4 Take the exit toward Jalan Hulu Kelang: Look for signs directing you to Jalan Hulu Kelang.
5 Follow Jalan Hulu Kelang: Continue on Jalan Hulu Kelang until you reach Boathouse Ampang.

Other Information

Information Details
Emergency Contact 03-9459526
Estimated Time By Car Approximately 30 to 45 minutes, depending on traffic conditions. The most direct route is via the AKLEH (Ampang-Kuala Lumpur Elevated Highway).
Estimated Time By Public Transportation (LRT) Take the Kelana Jaya Line LRT from Taman Bahagia station to Dato' Keramat station, then transfer to the Ampang Line to reach Ampang station. Estimated travel time: 41 minutes.
Notable Landmarks Along the Route
  • Mid Valley Megamall (Kuala Lumpur)
  • Kuala Lumpur City Centre (KLCC)
  • The Curve Petaling Jaya
